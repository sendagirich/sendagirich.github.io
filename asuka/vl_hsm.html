High School Musical

List of Characters

The Jocks

  • Troy Bolton- captain of the basketball team with a secret passion for singing
  • Chad Danforth- number two on the basketball team, Troy’s best friend
  • Zeke Baylor- basketball player with a secret passion for baking

The Thespians (Theater Kids)

  • Sharpay Evans- the “diva”, president of the drama club
  • Ryan Evans- Sharpay’s fraternal twin brother, vice-president of the drama club
  • Kelsi Nielsen- the shy composer of “Juliet and Romeo”, the schools drama club production
  • James- an earthworm expressionist
  • Susan- highly emotional drama queen
  • Cathy- a little too eager
  • Cyndra- has dreams of being an opera star

The Brainiacs (Science Club members)

  • Gabriella Montez- the shy new girl at school who is highly intelligent and has a secret passion for singing
  • Taylor McKessie- president of the Science Club
  • Martha Cox- has a secret passion for Hip-Hop dancing
  • Jack Scott- The schools PA announcer, the school’s DJ

The Skaters

  • Ripper- has a secret passion for the cello
  • Mongo- has absolutely no clue about anything

The Teachers

  • Ms. Darbus- the eccentric drama teacher
  • Coach Bolton- basketball coach, Troy’s dad.
  • Ensemble- Wildcat Cheerleaders, Jocks, Brainiacs, Thespians, Skater Dudes, Party Kids, Ms. Tenny (Science Teacher), Basketball Fans, Science Decathlon Moderator
