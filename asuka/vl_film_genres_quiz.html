Horror

These films have lots of monsters such as Frankenstein, or Dracula. These films make you scared and afraid.

Action

Films in which the heroes have lots of battles, do incredible stunts and drive fast.

Martial arts

These films have fighting such as kung-fu Judo, Karate, Taekwondo and so on. Bruce Lee made very famous martial arts films.

Adventure

These are like action films, but they take place in exotic places. These films include films about pirates, historical adventures such as sailing around the world and space exploration.

Comedy

There are many different types of these films. In general, these films make you laugh - a lot!

Romance

These films are love stories made to melt our hearts with stories of people finding each other and falling in love. Many of these films are romantic comedies.

Documentary

This is a film that investigates some real-life story that is very interesting for a number of reasons. Many of these films look at the causes of world problems or new types of scientific discoveries.

Animation

These films are sometimes cartoons such as Disney films. However, with computer animation, many cartoons are now animation films. These films use computer graphics to make elaborate stories of adventure, comedies, and more.

Disaster

These films are a type of adventure film. Unfortunately, these films focus on horrible things happening to us like end of the world films.

Superhero

These films are also a type of adventure film. These films feature superheroes from comic books such as Superman, Batman, and Spiderman.

Science-Fiction

These films are set in the future and might be about other planets, or just about the future of our planet Earth. these films often feature many elements of adventure films such as chases and battles.

Drama

These films are often sad stories about difficult situations in life such as fighting cancer or difficult love stories.

*Historical Drama

These films are based on real events that happened in the past that are historically important.

Thriller

These films are spy stories that are similar to adventure films, but often feature international spy rings, or countries trying to find out secrets about each other.

Detective story

These films focus on solving crimes. Usually, there is a detective who must find out who committed a crime before the criminal commits other horrible crimes.

Musical

These films have lots of songs and often dances.
