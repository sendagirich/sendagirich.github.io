04/11 Lesson 1

Lesson Aim:

  • Start watching The Sound of Music.
  • Learn Vocabulary for film genres.

Lesson

Watch The Sound of Music 1:
  1. Opening Scene: The film opens with Maria singing “The Sound of Music” on a hilltop.
  2. Abbey: Maria is late returning to the abbey.
  3. Maria Leaves the Abbey: Maria is informed by the Mother Abbess that she will leave the abbey to become a governess to Captain von Trapp’s children, setting the story in motion.
