4. Introduce shots Describing shots 1
  • materials
  • Scene: A continuous action or event in a single place and time, made of one or more shots, that tells a part of the story.
  • Shot: A single, continuous take from the camera, from the moment it starts recording to when it stops, representing a specific viewpoint or angle.
  • Frame: A single still image in a sequence of frames that, when played in rapid succession, creates the illusion of motion in film.
