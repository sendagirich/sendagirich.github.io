Part of a film to add in the edit

  • Titles / Opening credits.
    • Show the film title at the beginning of the film.
    • They are often accompanied by music.
    • In TV shows titles are usually part of an opening sequence.
  • Closing credits
    • List the cast and crew who made the film.
    • In your film write the names of your group. You can use nicknames.
  • Transitions
    • Transitions are how changes are made between shots and scenes.
  • Audio
    • Dialogue from the actors is matched with the shots and also music and sound effects.

3. What makes a good script?

Ask yourself and your group partners the following questions about The Sound of Music.
1. Who is it about?
  • Maria, a nun who leaves to be a governess and the Von Trapp family, who she goes to work for.
2. What did they want?
  • For Maria to marry Captain Von Trapp and live with the family.
3. Why can’t they get it?
  • Because the Captain is going to marry Baroness Shrader and Maria leaves.
4. What did they do about it?
  • Maria returns to the house and the Captain tells her he loves her.
Why doesn’t that work?
  • Because the Nazi’s want the Captain to go away to fight for them.
6. How does it end?
  • The family escape.
