1. Describe this shot.

A travelling establishing shot. The camera pans right across a picture of the school then tilts up to show the real school building.

2. Describe these shots.

Static over the shoulder (OTS) shot. The camera looks over Gabriella’s shoulder to focus on Troy’s face then switches for the second shot.

3. Describe this shot.

A tracking dolly shot. The camera tilts up from Gabriella’s feet to her face then dollies back to as she walks down the corridor.

4. Describe this shot.

A static long shot. A fixed camera position shows Troy as a small figure in the background practising basketball.

5. Describe this shot.

A tracking dolly or steadycam two shot. The camera moves back to follow Troy and Chad as they walk down the corridor.
