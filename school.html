Assessment

Sample questions

  1. What is your favourite subject? Why?
  2. What subjects don’t you like? Why?
  3. What places have you been to in school today?
  4. When is lunch time / 1st period…?
  5. What did / will you study in the third period?
  6. What is the easiest / most difficult / subject?
  7. What is the most interesting subject?
  8. Where do you study P.E.?
  9. Who teaches geography?
  10. What subject does Ms./Mr….teach?
  11. Where do you study math?
  12. Can you drink water in lessons?
  13. Can you use a smartphone in lessons?
  14. Do you like math? Why? / Why not?
  15. Do you have to wear a uniform in school?
  16. When do you usually leave school?
  17. What is your favourite day at school? Why?
  18. What is your least favourite day at school? Why?
