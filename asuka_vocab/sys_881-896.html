Text 1 (Narrative)

Last summer, a rescue team saved hikers from a mountain. The hikers had to beg to the team for help. The team define as “emergency” any situation where life is in danger. Their decision to go at night was hard. They needed to convey the danger through radios. To sustain their energy, they ate high-energy bars. They had to purchase new ropes before the trip. As the sun set, their hope didn’t fade away. They learned to regulate their breathing. They would distribute supplies evenly. Good weather would enhance their visibility. At base, they would chat with each other. The number of hikers didn’t exceed the team’s limit. They used snow to wipe their faces. They decided to cooperate with the local police. The leader’s courage seemed to inherit from his father.
