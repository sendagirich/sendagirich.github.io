Text 1 (Adventure story)

Last year, a team of explorers travelled to a remote island. They wanted to investigate strange lights that occur at night. During their trip, a terrible storm arose from the sea. It was very hard to cope with the strong wind and rain. The explorers had to endure many cold, wet nights. One day, they found a cave which they could transform into a shelter. Inside, they discovered an old chest full of gold coins. “I declare this treasure found!” shouted the leader. “This will alter our lives,” said another. They had to distinguish the real gold from fake stones. “Do not hesitate to take the coins,” said the leader. They needed to bury most of the treasure to hide it. Finally, they concluded their adventure by bringing a few coins home. They felt they had accomplished something great. They had defeated the storm and found treasure.

  1. What did the leader shout when they found the gold?
  2. How would finding the gold change the explorers’ lives?
  3. Where did the terrible storm come from?
  4. What could the explorers turn the cave into?
  5. What did the explorers defeat during their trip?
  6. Why did the explorers travel to the remote island?
  7. What did the explorers need to separate from fake stones?
  8. What did the explorers do with most of the treasure?
  9. What was difficult for the explorers to deal with?
  10. When do the strange lights happen on the island?
  11. What did the explorers feel they had achieved?
  12. What did the leader tell the explorers not to be slow about doing?
  13. What kind of nights did the explorers have to live through?
  14. How did the adventure finish?

Cloze Questions:

  1. “I __ this treasure found!” shouted the leader. (declare)

  3. A terrible storm __ the sea. (arise from / arose from)

  5. They had __ the storm. (defeat)
  6. They wanted to __ strange lights. (investigate)
  7. They had to __ the real gold __ fake stones. (distinguish…from)
  8. They needed to __ most of the treasure. (bury)
  9. It was very hard to __ the strong wind. (cope with)
  10. Strange lights __ at night. (occur)
  11. They felt they had __ something great. (accomplish)
  12. “Do not __ to take the coins,” said the leader. (hesitate to)

  14. Finally, they __ their adventure. (conclude)

Text 2 (Dialogue / Everyday Problem)

  • Anna: I can’t cope with all this mess! How can we accomplish anything here?
  • Ben: Don’t worry. First, let’s investigate what’s in these boxes. I declare today a “cleaning day”!
  • Anna: OK. But a problem might arise from putting everything away without a plan.
  • Ben: True. Let’s distinguish items we need from things to throw away. Do not hesitate to decide.
  • Anna: I think we can transform this room into a nice office. But we must endure a few hours of work.
  • Ben: Yes. After we bury all the rubbish in the bin, we’ll feel great. A clean room will alter our mood.
  • Anna: Good. What if a problem occur while we work?
  • Ben: Then we will defeat it together. I conclude this is the best plan. Let’s start!

Text 3 (Science News Report)

Scientists investigate changes in the Arctic. They conclude that ice is melting faster. This change can arise from warmer ocean temperatures. To cope with this, researchers try new methods. They want to transform sea water into fresh water for melting ice. They hope to accomplish a big goal: to slow down ice loss. Some scientists declare that we must act now. “We cannot hesitate to act,” one expert said. The ice will not easily defeat us if we work together. We must distinguish good ideas from bad ones quickly. We may need to bury some plans that don’t work. The changes will alter life for animals. “Polar bears endure difficult times,” they say. These changes occur faster every year.

Text 4 (Fairy Tale)

A little village had to cope with a dangerous dragon. It would occur every full moon. “I declare we must fight!” said the brave knight. But the people hesitate to attack. They could not defeat the beast alone. One wise woman said, “This problem arise from an old curse. We must investigate its source.” They found a magic stone that could transform a person into a hero. To accomplish the task, the knight used the stone. He had to endure a terrible battle. He managed to alter the dragon’s heart. He did not kill it but changed it. He learned to distinguish the monster’s anger from its sadness. Then, they bury the old curse under a tree. They conclude that kindness is stronger than fire.
