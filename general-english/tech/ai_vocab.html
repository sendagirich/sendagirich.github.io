Making example sentences

Prompt

  • Write 5 example sentences for each of the following words:
  • change
  • learn
  • help
  • need
  • live
  • ask
  • enjoy
  • wait
  • cook
  • talk

Output

change

  1. I need to change my clothes before we go out.
  2. The weather can change quickly in the mountains.
  3. She decided to change her career path.
  4. Can you change this $20 bill into smaller bills?
  5. The company plans to change its logo next year.

learn

  1. Children learn new languages more easily than adults.
  2. I want to learn how to play the guitar.
  3. We can learn from our mistakes.
  4. He is learning to code in his free time.
  5. She learned about the history of Rome in school.

help

  1. Can you help me carry these bags?
  2. This medicine should help with your headache.
  3. She always helps her neighbors with their groceries.
  4. The tutorial will help you understand the software.
  5. We need to help those in need.

need

  1. You need to drink more water.
  2. The plants need sunlight to grow.
  3. I need a new pair of shoes.
  4. We need to finish this project by Friday.
  5. He doesn’t need any assistance.

live

  1. They live in a small apartment downtown.
  2. I want to live near the ocean someday.
  3. She lives a very active lifestyle.
  4. He hopes to live to be 100 years old.
  5. We used to live in Canada before moving here.

ask

  1. Can I ask you a question?
  2. She asked for directions to the nearest station.
  3. He asked his boss for a raise.
  4. Don’t hesitate to ask if you need anything.
  5. They asked us to join them for dinner.

enjoy

  1. I really enjoy listening to jazz music.
  2. Did you enjoy the movie last night?
  3. She enjoys spending time with her family.
  4. We hope you enjoy your stay at the hotel.
  5. He enjoys hiking in the mountains on weekends.

wait

  1. Please wait here until I return.
  2. We had to wait in line for over an hour.
  3. She is waiting for her exam results.
  4. Don’t wait too long to make a decision.
  5. They waited patiently for the bus to arrive.

cook

  1. My father loves to cook Italian food.
  2. Can you cook dinner tonight?
  3. She is learning how to cook healthy meals.
  4. We often cook together on weekends.
  5. He cooked a delicious meal for his guests.

talk

  1. We need to talk about your performance.
  2. She talks to her friends every day.
  3. He talked for hours about his travels.
  4. Let’s talk more about this tomorrow.
  5. They were talking quietly in the corner.

Making texts

Prompt

  • Write a 500 word text that contains all of the words below. Write for a CEFR level A2 English learner. Write 5 comprehension questions for the texts. List the answers seperately below the questions:
  • change
  • learn
  • help
  • need
  • live
  • ask
  • enjoy
  • wait
  • cook
  • talk

Output

My New Life in Canada

My name is Ana. I moved to Canada last year. My life had a big change. I need to learn English. It is important for my new life.

Every day, I go to an English class. My teacher is very nice. She likes to help all the students. I ask her many questions. She always answers me. I enjoy my class very much.

After class, I go home. I live in a small apartment. I cook dinner for my family. Sometimes, I talk to my neighbors. They are friendly. I must wait for the bus every morning. The bus is sometimes late, but that is okay.

My English is getting better. I can talk to more people now. I enjoy my new life in Canada. It is a good change for me and my family.

Comprehension Questions:

  1. Where does Ana live now?
  2. What does Ana need to learn?
  3. Who helps Ana with her questions?
  4. What does Ana do after her English class?
  5. How does Ana feel about her new life?

Answers:

  1. She lives in Canada.
  2. She needs to learn English.
  3. Her teacher helps her.
  4. She goes home and cooks dinner for her family.
  5. She enjoys it. / She feels happy about it.
