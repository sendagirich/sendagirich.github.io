List Japanese kanji and kana for vocabulary for family.

*Kanji & Kana for Family Vocabulary:

  • Family – 家族 (かぞく, kazoku)
  • Parents – 両親 (りょうしん, ryōshin)
  • Mother – 母 (はは, haha) / お母さん (おかあさん, okāsan)
  • Father – 父 (ちち, chichi) / お父さん (おとうさん, otōsan)
  • Older Sister – 姉 (あね, ane) / お姉さん (おねえさん, onēsan)
  • Older Brother – 兄 (あに, ani) / お兄さん (おにいさん, oniisan)
  • Younger Sister – 妹 (いもうと, imōto)
  • Younger Brother – 弟 (おとうと, otōto)
  • Grandmother – 祖母 (そぼ, sobo) / おばあさん (obāsan)
  • Grandfather – 祖父 (そふ, sofu) / おじいさん (ojīsan)
  • Wife – 妻 (つま, tsuma) / 家内 (かない, kanai)
  • Husband – 夫 (おっと, otto) / 主人 (しゅじん, shujin)
  • Child – 子供 (こども, kodomo)
  • Daughter – 娘 (むすめ, musume)
  • Son – 息子 (むすこ, musuko)
  • Siblings – 兄弟 (きょうだい, kyōdai)
  • Uncle – 叔父/伯父 (おじ, oji)
  • Aunt – 叔母/伯母 (おば, oba)

Familiy

List Japanese kanji and kana for vocabulary to refer to one’s own family members then how to refer to another person’s family members.

*One’s Own Family (内 uchi):

  • 父 chichi (own father)
  • 母 haha (own mother)
  • 夫 otto (own husband)
  • 妻 tsuma (own wife)
  • 息子 musuko (own son)
  • 娘 musume (own daughter)
  • 兄弟 kyōdai (own brothers/siblings)
  • 姉妹 shimai (own sisters)

Another’s Family (外 soto):

  • お父さん otōsan (another’s father)
  • お母さん okāsan (another’s mother)
  • ご主人 goshujin (another’s husband)
  • 奥さん okusan (another’s wife)
  • 息子さん musukosan (sanother’s son)
  • 娘さん musumesan (another’s daughter)
  • ご兄弟 gokyōdai (another’s brothers/siblings)
  • ご姉妹 goshimai (another’s sisters)

Note: Use humble forms (e.g., 父) for your own family and respectful forms (e.g., お父さん) for others.
