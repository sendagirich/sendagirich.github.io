Example

John:     How was your weekend?
Ann:     It was good.
John     Really? What did you do?
Ann:     I played tennis on Saturday morning.
John:     I see. Sounds fun. How about in the evening?
Ann:     I went to my friend’s house and we ate sukiyaki.
John:     Sukiyaki? Sounds delicious! How about Sunday?
Ann:     Well, I watched TV and read a book in the morning and then studied English in the afternoon.
John:     Really? Sounds great! I studied English too! Do you like any English books, songs or movies?
Ann:     Yes, I love the Harry Potter books and movies. Anyway, how was your weekend.
John:     Well, I played soccer on Saturday but I fell and hurt my leg.
Ann:     Oh no! Are you OK?
John:     Yes, but I had to stop playing the game and bandage my leg.
Ann:     Sounds tough! I hope your Sunday was better.
John:     It was. I studied English like you and read a book about Robin Hood.
Ann:     I see. Sounds interesting. Who Robin Hood?
John:     Blah, blah, blah…….
